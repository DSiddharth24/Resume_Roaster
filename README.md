# Resume Roaster 🔥

A brutal, no-nonsense resume analyzer that acts as an experienced, sharp, and slightly cynical senior hiring manager. No participation trophies, no sterile templates, and no generic tips. Just blunt, highly specific, and funny-but-useful feedback on exactly what's holding your resume back—complete with high-impact, professional rewrites you can copy-paste straight into your document.

Developed with a bold, distinctive **Brutalist / Creative Tool** design aesthetic.

---

## ⚡ The Core Concept

Friends are too polite, and standard articles about "formatting tips" are too vague. The **Resume Roaster** tells you the honest truth. It plays the role of a hiring manager who has scanned over 50,000 resumes and is done wasting time with corporate fluff. 

The feedback is divided into four distinct phases:
1. **The Verdict**: A sharp, headline-style sentence summing up the resume's biggest problem. Perfect for a screenshot to send to a friend.
2. **The Six-Second Test**: An honest simulation of what a recruiter's eye actually registers in the first fleeting seconds of glancing at your document.
3. **Surgical Callouts**: A direct contrast grid showing the weak phrasing from your resume, why it fails, and an optimized, high-impact rewrite.
4. **The One Good Thing**: A genuine, specific compliment about something that genuinely works well, ensuring you walk away energized rather than demoralized.

---

## 🛠️ Features

- **Dual-Mode Submission**: Drag and drop your `.pdf` / `.txt` resume files, or paste raw plain text directly.
- **Interactive Scanning Simulator**: Experience a live simulated "scanned eye" tracking engine with ticker comments reflecting recruiter thoughts as they parse your resume.
- **Visual Contrast Grid**: Highly readable, side-by-side contrast boxes showing "Original Phrasing" versus "High-Impact Rewrite".
- **Instant Copy Utilities**: Copy the entire set of rewrites, the official verdict, or individual lines with a single click.
- **Brutalist Aesthetic**: Built on high-contrast, bold layouts, thick borders, neo-brutalist custom shadows, and custom marquee animations.

---

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React (v19) + TypeScript + Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (v4)
- **Animations**: Motion (formerly Framer Motion)
- **Backend Service**: Express Server proxying requests to Gemini API
- **AI Engine**: Gemini 3.5 Flash utilizing custom structured JSON responses
